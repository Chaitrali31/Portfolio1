Smile Day 4.0
Visual metaphor · Iterative format · Optimized for Instagram Reels / Facebook / WhatsApp
+
The Challenge
Create a feel-good, shareable World Smile Day moment that doesn't feel forced or overly promotional for a financial services brand.
The Insight
A smile is universal. The "road to happiness" metaphor connects naturally to vehicle ownership. Two wheels as eyes, a curved road as a smile — instant recognition.
The Result
Became a recurring campaign format (4.0 indicates iteration). High shareability due to visual simplicity and positive emotion. Works as static, GIF, or video.
